 - WHAT IS APPLE BODY TYPE


The apple body type is round and has wider shoulder line and flattish bottom , narrow hips. Little to undefined waistline  often apple tend to have slender legs and flat bottoms.


READ IF YOU ARE CONCERNED ABOUT
- WIDER MIDRIFF
- LACK OF WAISTLINE

HIGHLIGHTS
Define your waist because cloths that make the mid section narrower help achieve a more proportional body shape , invest in push up bra to lift your chest and always opt for slits at the waist.

CHARACTERISTICS
• Rounder, wider shoulder line and flattish bottom
• Average to big bust
• Ribcage and back appear somewhat wide
• Fullness around the mid section
• Narrow hip

HOW TO DRESS THE APPLE
An Apple body shape can appear top heavy due to full chest and upper back with little to no waist definition. In order to balance out the upper body choose cloths that add curves and fullness to the lower body to balance your silhouette

TOPS NECKLINE
The fabulous neckline for the apple body shape is low V necline. Embellishments and detail on and around the neckline are also great to draw the eye upward and away from the torso.

SLEEVES
Opt for loose, flared sleeves to build up volume on upper body. You can also play up shoulders with cap sleeves or shoulder pads, Avoid Flitted sleeves that do nothing for your arms.

JACKETS
Make sure the jacket is snug on the shoulder . Jackets should finish at hipbone to skim past the midsection and balance out the body frame. Avoid belts and double breasted jacket unless the belt is just under your bust.
