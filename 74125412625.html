PETITE STYLE TIPS AND SECRETS 

24th September 2019




PETITE GIRL OR WOMEN are those who are less then 5 feet. Petite is a French word that is translated into small or tiny in English literally . If you are short woman your best bet is to shop for petite style from clothing stores.
Petite girls have Narrower Shoulders , Shorter Torso & Shorter Limbs.

When I talk about myself so my height is 4 feet 2 inches , its very hard for me to find out my size clothing , what to wear and what should not wear . Quite honestly , when I first started the journey of finding my own style , I was the former .
First of all don't get me wrong I'm not one to restrict myself , if you call something a rule the first and most likely reaction from me is probably to rebel .


Break the Petite Style Rules Smartly

Short girls can only wear short skirts

The reason is that the mini skirts and mini dresses are universally flattering to petite women , they are not the only style you can wear and look good. The way to break this rule is to wear a maxi skirt. I'm talking about the dresses that reaches your ankles.

Stay away from Midi Dresses

The reason is simple , it ends at an awkward length at your calves between your knees and your ankles, so there is a big risk that your legs could look cut off.
How to break this rule, wear midi d style distract people from the horizontal line formed around you calves thus the people will not easily notice your legs cut - off. Seeing your legs skin of your legs will in fact make your proportion look better and longer.

Petite should only wear coats above the knee

While I agree coats above the knee or at the knee is the safest length for petite women, going longer can actually be very flattering if you master the skills. The way to break this rule is to go REALLY long, long enough to reach just a couple of inches above your ankle.
Wearing an oversized sweaters with high boots try to show out your little skin around thighs. 

IMPORTANT RULES
 
1 . Try wearing a skirt or dress at knee height or just above the knee, if you are confident showing your legs. And on your top half, wear a cropped jacket or shirt that is tucked in or fitted at the waist.
2 . Alternatively, try a longer cardigan or jacket in line with your skirt.vertical striped pattern can work wonders to create a longer line especially when selecting a pair of pants or skirt.
3 . Avoid very large or oversized bags if you have a smaller frame; select a bag size which is proportionate to you. 


ALL SAID AND DONE, THERE ARE NO HARD AND FAST RULES FOR FASHION, ONLY GUIDELINES , TIPS AND EXPERT ADVICE.
