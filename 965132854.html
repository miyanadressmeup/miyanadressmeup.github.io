MISTAKES EVERY STYLISH WOMEN DO

03rd February 2019



We all make mistakes ::: that much we know. And when it comes to fashion, all it takes is one look at an old photo to remind us of that fact. Because while trendsetting women give off the appearance of always getting it right, there are certain mistakes every woman can cop to. The good news is that acknowledging them is the first step.

Learning from the mistakes chic women have made can hopefully help eliminate the issues going forward. So read on for a little look into those things we all do (and then regret), and then shop a few of our favorite stylish pieces that'll help you overcome whatever your style obstacles may be.



NOT KNOWING HOW TO LAYER

Mixing and matching our favorite staples is sometimes easier said than done. Even with the best intentions, it can be tricky to nail the best way to layer. Our rule of thumb is don't be afraid to mix prints and textures; if it's looking a bit bulky, be sure to add a belt.


NOT GETTING ITEMS TAILORED

our busy lives, finding the time (and money) to drag things to the tailor can be a chore. But time and again, we've found that the results can mean the difference between a good piece and a great one.

 

WEAR SAME ITEM AGAIN AND AGAIN

Having hundreds of cloths in your wardrobe is not a good idea believe me we all should maintain a minimalistic wardrobe because multiple items in your wardrobe may lead you to wear same items repeatedly without even knowing , try to have less and best mix and match try everyday new with your same outfits but not same again and again


ALL SAID AND DONE, THERE ARE NO HARD AND FAST RULES FOR FASHION, ONLY GUIDELINES , TIPS AND EXPERT ADVICE.
