There Is Endless Street Style Inspiration for How to Make Ripped Jeans Look Chic

21st September 2019




No matter the season, denim is always the perfect go-to clothing item. After all, there are so many different styles, it can be dressed up or down and jeans pretty much go with anything. One of the trickiest types of denim to figure out, though, is definitely ripped jeans.




1: Pair dark ripped jeans with a polka dot blouse & bright heels
2: Pair black ripped denim overalls with an oversized tee
3: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a white tee & leather jacket
4: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a striped button down
5: Pair mid-wash ripped jeans with fishnet tights & a black tee
6: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a band tee & leather bag
7: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a neutral v-neck sweater
8: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a crop top, long cardigan & sneakers
9: Pair light-wash ripped jeans with a bralette & bomber jacket
10: Pair black ripped jeans with a crop graphic tee and small backpack


REMEMBER : Always prefer snickers with ripped jeans.
