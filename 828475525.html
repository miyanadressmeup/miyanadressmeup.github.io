WHAT IS CAPSULE WARDROBE & WHY CAPSULE WARDROBE ESSENTIALS?

 " OUR CONSUMERIST CULTURE & FASHION HAVE TRICKED US INTO BELIEVING THAT WE SHOULD OWN EVERYTHING "

2nd September 2019



When it comes to shopping for our new season wardrobe , we want to buy pieces that will last us for years to come and can be styled all year around.
With the rise of the capsule wardrobe and our desire to reduce the impact we are having on the planet with our FAST FASHION habits we turned to our editions and fashion experts to find out their wardrobe must have.


A CAPSULE WARDROBE IS COMPOSED BY A SMALL AMOUNT OF KEY ITEMS ADAPTED TO YOUR TASTE , THESE KEYS ITEMS ARE CAREFULLY CHOSEN TO COMBINE WITH EACH OTHER SO THAT IN THE END OF THE YEAR YOU HAVE THOUSANDS OF COMBINATIONS TO LAST FOR MANY OTHER MONTHS TO COME.

SO,  ITS REALLY IMPORTANT THAT YOU CHOOSE HIGH QUALITY PIECES .




The beauty of having a capsule wardrobe is that its good for the environment and as well as for you just consider , in your life in a lot of different ways it get easy to choose your cloths in the morning , easy to pack for trip , you will have more space in your closet and will provide you with a deep enjoyment feeling & motivation. to get any outfit from your own closet

 "MOST IMPORTANTLY , CAPSULE WARDROBE ARE ABOUT PERSONAL STYLE"
