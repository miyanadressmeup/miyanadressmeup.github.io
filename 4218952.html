THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT PROFESSIONAL PROFILE PICTURES

9th September 2019


Keep things natural

Make sure you remain approachable and consistent with your professional appearance. You shouldn’t look different in photos and in real life.

Don’t change up your look

It may be tempting to get all glammed up for a professional profile picture, but don’t stray from your normal routine on picture day. Yes, you want to look groomed and professional, so if you don’t normally brush your hair, please do that for everyone’s benefit, however if you don’t normally wear makeup, this is not the time to pile it on. Again, you should look the same in your picture as you do in real life.

Be conscious of the background

Find a neutral background that won’t distract the viewer. You want to be the focus point, not the background. Standing in front of a plain wall or simple gardens and greenery work well. If you can find a lot of open space where you can stand and have just the sky or far away landscape in the background, that’s also a good option.



Never use a selfie

Never use a selfie as your professional profile photo. Have someone else take a photo of you. It’s as simple as that. Selfies may be all the rage on Instagram and snapchat, but not in the professional world.

Professional profile pictures are important, so take the time to actually make your photo worth it.
