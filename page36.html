SPORTY LOOKS

PreviousNextClose
This style is rather an easy one to pick up and describe. It is all about sports. If you are a gym enthusiast or are someone who loves your morning runs, this style may have already been a part of you. In this style, girls will wear stuff from some popular sports companies such as Nike or Adidas with complementary runner shoes of some similar sports brands. The idea of this fashion is to flaunt the sports out of them. This is an effortless style as it does not involve running behind flashy stuff. Just some loose runner pants, basic tees, pony or open short hairs or even a bun.
