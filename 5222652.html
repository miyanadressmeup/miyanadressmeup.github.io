HOW TO ACCESSORIZE LIKE A PRO 

1st September 2019

When you are deciding how to accessorize your look there are few things you should keep in your mind , because accessories can either make or break your outfit. That should be the last thing that you should put together to put together with your outfit .




Here are few questions which you should have ask yourself before you get started :            


      -  what will go with what accessories             
      - does my outfit already contain statement of some sort before accessorizing
      - what are the color tones of my outfits
      - how many colors an I wearing
      - Am I wearing patterns or prints 

HERE ARE THE TIPS TO TUNE UP YOUR ACCESSORIES WITH WHAT OUTFIT

      White shirt with pants or denim tune it up with golden watch printed neckerchief & ankle strap sandal.

     Nude lipstick with black LBD , plums and long black earrings

     Prefer studs earrings with printed tee  


THE STATEMENT


    If your outfit already has a fun statement on its own then maybe keep your accessories to more of a minimum, If I'm wearing more of a     statement outfit then I personally like to go for simple more delicate jewelry . If your looks is more basic then you can really amp up your outfit     with some accessories .Think about adding some print , texture , or color through your accessories to bring in that statement to you look .


ALL SAID AND DONE, THERE ARE NO HARD AND FAST RULES FOR FASHION, ONLY GUIDELINES , TIPS AND EXPERT ADVICE.
