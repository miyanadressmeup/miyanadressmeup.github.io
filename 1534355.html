FIVE POINTS TO REMEMBER BEFORE DRESSING UP 

27th August 2019



Navigating the how and why of style can be tricky but with these simple stylist recommended tip you'll be a fashionista in no time

To feel good, you have to look good, but for a lot of individuals finding the proper attire for their body type, skin tone, etc., is a struggle. Moment you enter your wadrobe always remember Why The Way You Dress Is Important & Know Your Colors and Dress for Your Body Type .
In a professional environment, it’s perfectly acceptable to express your individuality. It’s okay to have a little fun with your outfits and when done correctly, it can make you more appealing to prospective clients. However, it is important to avoid dressing in ‘loud’ clothing, meaning avoid neon colors, animal print and other distracting patterns. And always remember Be Comfortable With Yourself .




Here are the 5 tips :

        - Find out if a pair of jeans fits without a trip to the dressing room

        - Make an outfit great with sunglasses , hand bag and smart pair of shoes

        - Weigh down a light scarf with earrings

        - Maximize your shoe collection

       -  make a basic hairstyle look way more impressive in your outfit  


ALL SAID AND DONE, THERE ARE NO HARD AND FAST RULES FOR FASHION, ONLY GUIDELINES , TIPS AND EXPERT ADVICE.
