WHAT IS RECTANGULAR BODY SHAPE ?

The rectangular body shape is characterized by equal bust, waist and hip measurements. Rectangles lend to be tall and lean. They are not particularly curvy, the waist is not well defined , and the bottom is rather flat.

HOW TO DRESS THE RECTANGLE

The upper and lower part of the rectangle body are well proportioned and create a balanced silhouette . The focus of this body shape should be on defining the waist by breaking up the rectangle. when enhancing your waist you have two options if you are very slender and went to create more curves you can proportionally add volume to your upper and lower body and leave the waist as it.
alternatively , you can make your wait appear slimmer by pulling it in but leaving the upper and lower body as is.


This means breaking up straight silhouette to create curves from waist up and down.
Invest in a good push up bra to lift your chest
wear well structure cloths
use prints , texture and details strategically to create interest
wear bright colors on your upper and lower body - but not around the waist
keep away from shapeless , oversized and too large items.

TOPS NECKLINES

SLEEVES
The right type of sleeves for the rectangle body is one that adds interest and volume to the upper or lower body depending on the sleeves length . Opt for rolled up , of cuffed sleeves, as these will drew attention. To create volume the sleeves should be loose and wide liked flared , princess , puff or flatter sleeves.
AVOID shapeless sleeves style  

SHIRT OR JACKETS

AVOID boxy style

DRESSES

A great dress for a rectangle body is one that is balanced on top and bottom whilst pulling in the waist
opt for dark colors around your waist or add a dark belt to a dress

JUMPSUITS & PLAY SUITS
