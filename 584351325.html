WHAT IS PEAR BODY ?
The pear body shape have large hips which are wider than the bust and shoulders. Other points are that they are a defined waist , and proportionately slim arms and shoulder.

CHARACTERISTICS OF THE PEAR
Shoulders are narrow than hips and may be sloped
small top half as well as bust
a defined waist thanks to larger hips
full hips and muscular weight gain tend to first be visible around

HOW TO DRESS
The pear shape is heavier on the bottom which creates an imbalance with the upper half of the body . The key to dress this body shape is to take attention away from the wider hips and to draw it towards the upper body and defined waist instead.

The highlights are given below :
focus on structured shoulders to visually wider the shoulder line
invest in a good push up bra
wear bright colors on your upper body
use eye catching print & textures
stay away from boxy tops that hide the waist

TOP NECKLINE
Dont wear narrow collar like cowl collar , lace trims and ruffles , this will unbalanced and make you lower look more heavy
DO wear wide collar and embellish to draw people eyes upwards
more exams of neckline : off shoulder, square , bateau, cowl & sabrina

SLEEVES
Dont wear unstructured sleeves
Do wear structured sleeves which are flared from shoulder and slim on wrist

SHIRTS , T-SHIRTS AND TOPS
When selecting tops, choose style that help balance the lower body because the pear body shape defined waist.
tops should not go beyond the hips , go for either crop tops or tops that reach the waist line
AVOID the detailing and pockets around the hips
In shirts avoid long fit hip details instead go for hip length buster

JACKETS
Similar to shirts , jackets for the pear shaped body should add volume to the bust and shoulders area while either avoiding the hip area altogether , or skimming over it. A cropped jacket will add structure and volume to the shoulder and bust area AVOID wearing the jackets which ends wright above the hip , prefer wearing the jackets which are mid-thigh length structured shoulders neckline embellishments.

BOTTOMS
The right bottoms will minimize the lower body and de-emphasis the hips so go for darker colours , clean line and simple stitching to avoid the attention to your lower body
In trousers mid-or high waist to hug in the definition
AVOID fitted trousers or we can say skinny once
